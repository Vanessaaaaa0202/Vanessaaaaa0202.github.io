About Me

关于我

I'm Vanessa Lei (雷杰童) — a Financial Data Analyst at the intersection of finance and technology, based in Los Angeles. I hold a BS in Financial Mathematics from Ohio State and an MS in Applied Data Science from USC, which means I genuinely speak both languages: P&L and Python, forecasting models and machine learning pipelines.

My path into FP&A wasn't accidental. Working across startups and nonprofits, I kept finding the same gap — finance teams drowning in manual processes, and data teams building tools that no one in the business could actually use. I became obsessed with closing that gap: automating the close cycle with VBA, surfacing deferred revenue risks months in advance with cohort models, turning a 40-tab spreadsheet into a Tableau dashboard that an executive can actually read in five minutes.

What makes me different is that I bring AI and automation fluency into financial workflows — using Python for cohort analysis and churn modeling, SQL for AR visibility, and tools like Power BI and Tableau to make financial storytelling fast and clear. I don't just run the numbers; I build the systems that make the numbers trustworthy.

我是雷杰童 Vanessa，财务数据分析师，坐落在金融与技术的交叉点上，目前在洛杉矶。我本科主修金融数学（俄亥俄州立大学），硕士攻读应用数据科学（南加大）——这意味着我同时说两种语言：P&L和Python，预测模型和机器学习管道。

走向FP&A并非偶然。在初创公司和非营利机构工作的过程中，我反复发现同一个痛点：财务团队被手动流程淹没，而数据团队构建的工具业务侧根本用不上。我开始专注于填补这个鸿沟：用VBA自动化月结流程、用队列模型提前数月预警递延收入风险、把密密麻麻的电子表格变成高管五分钟就能读懂的Tableau仪表盘。

我的独特之处在于将AI和自动化能力带入财务工作流——用Python做队列分析和流失预测，用SQL提升应收账款可见度，用Power BI和Tableau让财务叙事变得快速清晰。我不只是跑数字，我搭建让数字变得可信的系统。
